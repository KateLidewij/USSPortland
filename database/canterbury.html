USS Canterbury


USS Canterbury NCC-75505 is an Intrepid Class Light Explorer. It was constructed at Antares Ship Yards and was commissioned in 2380. In late 2384, following its first refit, it became the second command of Captain T’Lisa Anderson.


During the last 8 years the crew and the ship have faced many challenges including being part of a combined Klingon, Cardassian and Federation fleet tasked with the destruction of a Borg Unicomplex and the retrieval of an abducted Starfleet Captain.


Its current senior staff consists of:


  • T’Lisa Anderson as Commanding Officer
  • Caelena t’Drusa as Executive Officer
  • Kror Gar’Rel as Chief Sec/Tac
  • Chastity Muller as Chief Operations
  • Richard Dalziel as Chief Engineer
  • Emily Dalziel M.D. as Chief Medical
  • Dran Retol as Chief Science Officer
  • Ghol Thelan as Chief Flight Officer
  • Greta Sorenson as Marine CO
  • Andrew Dalziel as Chief of the Boat
