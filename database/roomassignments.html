USS Portland

Room numbers

and assigned Quarters

Room numbers are build up starting with the deck number, then the row on the deck followed by the room number. So room number 4A4 is the fourth room on deck 4, row A.

The rows are concentric circles which take up the entire saucer section, and are numbered from A to F, starting in the center. Ergo, row F is the outermost row, and row A is the innermost row. Not all deck have A-E, it depends on the size of the deck. The room numbers start at the stern of the ship and go counter clockwise.

VIP room numbers are A through F, so they have two letters in the room designation, one for the row and one for the room number.
