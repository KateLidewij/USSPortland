USS Portland

Lounges/Messhalls

Enlisted Crewmen’s Mess Hall:

The enlisted crewmen’s mess hall is located on deck 6, along with the enlisted crew quarters. It is a triangular shaped room with food synthesizers in the middle. The room offers many tables, each with four chairs, spread out over the whole room. In the middle of the area is the main staircase.

Officers’ Mess Hall:

Located in the center of deck 5 is the circular officer’s mess hall. In the middle of the room are two food replicators. Along the room’s outer wall are ten four-persons table and chair.

VIP Lounge:

Located in the center of deck 4 us the circular shaped VIP Lounge. This is a place for the ship’s VIP’s/Guest to relax. It has two comm stations, a head, a food synthesizer and 4 groups of chairs and couches. It is also equipped with holographic projectors. While the ship is not carrying VIPs, it can be used by senior staff for meetings or recreation.

Senior Staff Lounge:

The senior staff lounge is open to the command staff and all senior staff. The lounge extends through two decks, with the main level on deck 3 and a balcony on deck 2. On the balcony are two seating arrangements. On the lower level are multiple seating arrangements. Under the balcony (hidden on the plans) is a food synthesizer. The room has holographic projectors and a retractable viewscreen, so any kind of activity can be held in the lounge.

Forward Viewing Gallery:

Located on deck 9 are three viewing galleries. These galleries give you a nice view of space. You can sit here on one of the three couches and relax while you see the stars go by.

Viewport:

Viewports are the windows of the ship. Some of them offer swivel chairs and a small table, which creates a perfect location for your morning coffee or a light nap.
