USS Quebec City


The USS Quebec City was a Miranda-class vessel active in Starfleet service in the 24th century.

In the late 23rd century, the Quebec City was under the command of Captain Algard Trebonne.

By 2391, the hull of the USS Quebec City was nearing the end of its useful life. The Quebec City was thus rigged for remote control and used as a target drone. It was in this capacity that the Quebec City was used for the testing of Project Mongoose.

During testing, the USS Portland and USS Fox responded to a distress call and came under attack by Orion pirates. While both the Portland and the Orion cruiser were disabled by Project Mongoose and the massive power drain associated with it, a number of crew members from the USS Portland, including Timothy Rouse, Tyrlai Zade and Jason Beauvoir, used the Quebec City to turn the tide of the battle and destroy the Orion cruiser, with a little assistance from Captain Trebonne and his crew via temporal fluctuations.
