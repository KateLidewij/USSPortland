USS Portland

Recreation Deck

The USS Portland has a two-deck Recreation Deck. On the upper deck are several seating areas with food synthesizers, library consoles and viewports. There is also a bar with 7 stools and the holographic simulation room, better known as the holodeck. In the floor of the upper level are three open areas, surrounded by glass railing, that lets you look down to the lower level.

On the upper deck is also the galley, which serves fresh foods during alpha and beta shift hours. The galley is located on the side near the tables.

The lower level is reachable through a spiral staircase from the upper level, besides the normal doors to the hallway.

On this level you can find the swimming pool, a Jacuzzi and a gymnasium. There is a sound- and waterproof barrier in between the pool and gymnasium. The gym will, besides recreational use, also be used by physical therapy or the security department for various trainings. Next to the pool are two changing rooms.

The other half of the lower deck is for entertainment. There are multiple programmable game tables, video game consoles and a virtual reality system. Along the outer wall are multiple viewports located.

Both levels of the recreation deck are outfitted with holographic projectors. These are used to display the rec room’s staff. Except for the position of Recreation officer, an enlisted who is responsible for everything related to the area. The rest of the staff is holographic, the bartender, the chef, the waiter, the lifeguard, the game host, and instructors for the pool and gymnasium. Off course they’re not active all the time, but only when needed.
